Project Overview

We were preparing to return to the office as the option for remote work was coming to an end.

A policy was drafted requiring proof of vaccination in order to return to the office. We were on a very short deadline, and needed a way for staff to securely submit vaccination cards to HR staff for review.

Project duration April 2021 - April 2021

My role within this project is that of a Lead UX designer, UX Researcher, UX Tester, Database Designer, Developer and Tester.

User Research

I relied on research of other submission applications which allowed users to upload documents. I also focused on security and ease of use by staff. This would need to be easy for staff to create submissions and allow HR staff to deny, reject or allow users to re-submit if needed. This also needed to be completed within an extremely short deadline, 1 week.

Pain Points

Upload Document

Not all staff will have access to a scanner

Secure

Staff who review submissions should not download the submission to their computer by default, for security reasons

Confusion

Staff were not willing to deal with a complicated submission process

Wireframes

Drafting iterations of each screen allowed me to focus on the pain points identified and confirm the layout would address the presented issues.

Due to the expedited deadline I didn't have much time to expand on multiple options. So I kept it basic and straight forward.

Digital Wireframes

Hi-fidelity Mockups

Make the link obvious for users to submit their information. Also provide a button for Admins to approve submissions.

Usability Study Findings

Review

Admins had trouble locating button to access the Admin review section.

Links

Users weren’t aware “Provide Information” was a link.

Launch

I only had enough time for some quick in-house testing with a handful of co-workers before presenting the website to our CTO to review with HR and give me some feedback. Due to the urgent deadline, HR started using it immediately. Both HR and our CTO were happy with it's performance as they were able to review over 3,000 staff submissions. HR later requested to expand on this project to begin logging other staff information.

Take Aways

Impact

The ease of use and ability to use a mobile phone camera to submit a Covid vaccine card made adoption much greater. HR was able to process over 3,000 submissions and make the transition back into office easy for staff.

What I Learned

This was a project with little to no runway. I wasn’t afforded much time to think through workflows or work with end users. I was able to leverage prior knowledge to help develop this application within a week and publish to production for use.
