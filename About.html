About Me

I’m Ramiro Puentes, a Software Engineer & Systems Integrations Engineer with over 10 years of experience in application development, system implementation, and IT analysis. My expertise lies in full-stack development, with a focus on .NET, C#, SQL, and cloud-based integrations, alongside a strong passion for AI-driven solutions.

Throughout my career, I have designed and implemented critical web applications that enhance user experience, accessibility, and performance. My projects range from faculty and student management systems to enterprise-grade applications for HR departments, election services, and county-wide platforms. Notable contributions include:

  • Developing an orientation web app for new students, ensuring accessibility and user-friendliness.
  • Leading the Faculty Roster App—an essential tool for instructional staff to manage courses and students
  • Creating HR applications for preferred names, pronouns, and COVID vaccination tracking
  • Optimizing Fresno County IT applications, including website migrations, UX/UI improvements, and CMS integrations.

Beyond development, I thrive in collaborative environments, successfully coordinating with large teams across multiple campuses to drive innovation and efficiency. My approach prioritizes scalability, security, and intuitive user experiences, backed by certifications in Google UX Design, Software Project Management, and Deep Learning.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy researching AI advancements, mentoring junior developers, and contributing to open-source projects. Let’s connect and explore how I can help solve complex problems with technology!
