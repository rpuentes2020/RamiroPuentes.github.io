Project Overview

Human Resources was looking for a way to allow Staff to submit a "Preferred" name as well as a pronoun.

I was looking forward to the opportunity to build something that helps staff provide this information in an easy decisive way.

Project duration September 2022 - October 2022

My role within this project is that of a Lead UX designer, UX Researcher, UX Tester and Developer.

User Research

User research was conducted as an unmoderated usability study which focused on existing school sites that provide this service. I began looking into any feedback provided by users for the included sites.

Most of the feed back was positive however the notable issues expressed by users were

  • Trouble finding the correct page
  • Unclear on the status of the submission
  • Lack of feedback from their submission, no response when approved
